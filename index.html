Nigeria

Ebonyi State, is located in southeastern Nigeria, is known for its rich cultural heritage, agricultural strength, and fast-growing economy. Its capital, Abakaliki, serves as a major commercial hub, especially for rice, yam, and cassava production. The state is home to beautiful landscapes, including the rolling hills of the Ugeleke and the popular Salt Lake in Okposi, which highlights its historical salt mining tradition. Ebonyi is also recognized for its diverse ethnic groups, predominantly the Igbo people, and its commitment to infrastructural development and education.
